About Us

At Chilli Bounty, we are passionate about serving delicious rice bowls and refreshing drinks. Our humble beginnings stem from a desire to deliver authentic flavors that delight every palate.

Each bowl and beverage is crafted with fresh, premium ingredients and our unique spice blends, offering a satisfying experience with every bite and sip.
